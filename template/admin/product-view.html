Description

Crafted in our Swiss atelier, the Heritage Gold Watch reimagines a classic tradition with meticulous detail and modern engineering. Every component is finished by hand — from the sun-brushed dial to the polished case-back inscription.

The 41 mm 18K rose-gold case houses a 25-jewel automatic movement with 60-hour power reserve. The hand-stitched alligator strap is finished with a deployant clasp, while the domed sapphire crystal carries an anti-reflective coating on both sides.
