Frequently Asked

Quick answers, crunchy facts.

Found something that doesn’t fit here? Drop us a message above — we love a good question.

Where do you deliver?

We currently ship to 120+ cities across India through our courier partners. International shipping is available for select countries — get in touch for a custom quote.

Do you offer wholesale & bulk pricing?

Absolutely. For orders over 50 kg we offer custom pricing, branded packaging, and dedicated account managers. Use the enquiry form above and select “Wholesale & Bulk Orders”.

How long does Bhujia Bite stay fresh?

Every pack is nitrogen-flushed and stays crispy-fresh for 6 months unopened. Once opened, we recommend finishing within 7 days for the perfect crunch.

Are your products vegetarian?

Yes, every single one. All Bhujia Bite products are 100% vegetarian and use only pure desi ghee — no palm oil, no animal fats, no preservatives.

Can I customise hampers for corporate gifting?

Yes — from logo-printed boxes to mixed flavour selections, we love crafting bespoke hampers. Festive bookings open 6 weeks before each major festival.

What is your return / refund policy?

If your packet arrives damaged or stale (it shouldn’t!), we’ll replace it free of charge or refund within 7 days of delivery. Just email us with a photo of the unboxing.
